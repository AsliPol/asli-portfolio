Audit Findings — 5 Issues Identified
Broad match keywords consuming 61% of budget with low intent
Keywords

The account is running broad match on high-volume, low-intent queries like "office space" and "workspace" — pulling in irrelevant traffic from users searching for home office furniture, real estate listings, and remote work tips. This is the single largest driver of wasted spend.

KeywordMatch TypeClicksCostLeadsCPL
office spaceBroad842$1,1803$393
workspace near meBMM310$4284$107
coworking TorontoPhrase188$2609$29
flexible office rentalExact94$1327$19
Recommendation
Pause all broad match keywords. Migrate top spend to phrase and exact match equivalents. Add 40+ negative keywords including: "home office", "furniture", "virtual office", "free coworking", "office for sale".
↑ High impact · Est. $520/mo saved
No conversion tracking on contact form — reporting blind
Tracking

The primary lead form on /contact is not tagged. Google Ads is optimizing toward "page views" as a proxy conversion — meaning the algorithm is bidding to bring visitors, not leads. Smart bidding is completely miscalibrated.

Conversion ActionStatusFires?Priority
Contact form submitMissingNoCritical
Phone call clickMissingNoCritical
Tour booking page viewPartialInconsistentHigh
Landing page sessionActiveYesLow value
Recommendation
Implement GA4 + Google Tag Manager form submission tracking on all 3 lead forms. Set up phone call click tracking via GTM click listener. Once 30+ conversions/month accumulate, switch bidding from Manual CPC to Target CPA at $75 initial target.
↑ High impact · Enables smart bidding
Landing page sends all ad traffic to homepage — 78% bounce rate
Landing Page

Every campaign is pointing to the homepage. Users clicking "coworking space for startups" land on a generic page with 6 navigation options, no lead form above the fold, and copy that doesn't match their search intent. Message match is broken.

PageAvg. TimeBounce RateForm ViewsSubmissions
Homepage (current)0:3878%12%1.4%
Dedicated LP (benchmark)1:5238%64%8–12%
Recommendation
Build 2 dedicated landing pages — one for "coworking / hot desks" searches, one for "private office / team" searches. Each page: headline mirrors ad copy, single CTA (Book a Tour), no nav menu, social proof (occupancy rate, logos), form above fold. Target: reduce bounce to <45%.
↑ Med impact · +4–6 leads/mo est.
Ad schedule running 24/7 — 31% of spend outside business hours
Budget

Ads are running around the clock including weekends. Analysis of conversion data shows 94% of form submissions occur Mon–Fri 8am–7pm. $992/month is being spent on zero-conversion time windows.

Recommendation
Restrict ad schedule to Mon–Fri 7:30am–7:30pm. Apply -50% bid adjustment on evenings. Reallocate the $992/mo recovered budget to peak hours (Mon–Wed 9am–1pm show highest CVR).
↑ Med impact · $992/mo reallocated
Competitor brand terms unprotected — 340 impressions/mo lost
Opportunity

Three direct competitors are capturing branded searches for "FlexDesk" — users who already know the brand and are close to converting. These searches have near-zero competition and extremely low CPC.
