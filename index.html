My Journey

Career Story

My career began in 2016 in international financial operations. For over three years, I worked as a Destination Specialist and Sales & Accounts Reconciliation Specialist at one of Europe's leading tourism companies — managing 500,000+ daily transactions and building structured reports for international teams.

That experience trained me to think in attribution, accuracy, and data patterns. The exact same discipline that makes campaigns measurable and budgets accountable.

I chose to pivot into marketing deliberately — because I saw how much sharper a marketer can be when they actually understand the data behind the decisions. My Postgraduate Diploma in Business Marketing (3.9 GPA, Dean's Honour Roll) gave me the creative and strategic toolkit to match that foundation.

My dream is to join a team that values curiosity, moves fast, and wants a marketer who can read a dashboard as fluently as they write a brief.
